New Approach to Daanish Education

The Balochistan Daanish Schools and Centres of Excellence Authority is dedicated to educating and grooming children in remote and underdeveloped areas of balochistan by providing quality education. The Government has extended the necessary financial and administrative support, aiming to enhance the current system to make the Authority economically viable and financially sustainable, while increasing the number of beneficiaries. I am pleased with the efforts of the Minister for School Education, Secretary of the School Education Department, and the Officials and Staff of the BDS&CE Authority in achieving and implementing the objectives set by the Government to steer the Authority in a new direction
